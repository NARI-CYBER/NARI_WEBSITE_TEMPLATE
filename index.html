Executive Cyber Security

This one-day workshop is designed for executive leadership to become familiarized with common and obscure threats that are a danger to any industry, covering building control system incident response as well as topics specifically targeted to today’s decision-making executives

Network Analysis

The Network Analysis Workshop is a great place to start, covering the basics of Information Technology and Operational Technology including:

  • Basic networks
  • Firewalls
  • Use of GrassMarlin
  • Use of Wireshark

Gain hours of hands-on experience and essential skills for analyzing network traffic on Information Technology (IT) and Operational Technology (OT) networks

Building Control System - Incident Response

These exercises address the fundamentals of building control systems including:

  • Triage
  • Incident Response
  • Network Hunting
  • Active OT Network Defense

This course incorporates information from the Executive Cyber Security course to give a well-rounded look at Building Control Systems security while providing students with real-world experience in the fundamentals of BCS incident response on BACnet-based systems and networks. Teamwork and communication are required to pass the final exercise.

Hospital Control System Incident Response Course

All the concepts taught in our Building Control Systems course are covered here tailored with an emphasis on technology found in the healthcare facilities. While this course is recommended for professionals in the medical industry, all of the concepts taught easily translate to any building control system.
